# DevRel Summit 2024: Field Notes

Three themes kept surfacing throughout the summit:

## 1. Cohorts over campaigns

Developer relations teams are moving from one-off launches to cohort-based education. Think multi-week learning circles with built-in accountability, not just a single webinar.

## 2. AI enablement, not AI demos

Almost every hallway conversation focused on guiding customers on how to adopt AI responsibly—not just showcasing shiny AI features. We need more “how do I run this internally?” playbooks.

## 3. Success = retention

The metric conversation is finally shifting toward retention and product-qualified advocacy. The best talks shared dashboards tracking time-to-first-success plus community stickiness.

I left energized by how transparent practitioners are being about what works (and what doesn’t). Let me know if you want the longer notes.
