# Civic Signal Map: Visualizing Policy Momentum

I needed a way to see how AI and compute governance proposals flowed through committees, agencies, and think tanks without relying on disconnected spreadsheets. Civic Signal Map is my answer: a filterable, zoomable interface that shows who is shaping the conversation and where public comment opportunities appear.

## Why a map?

Policy conversations leave traces—press releases, hearings, procurement requests. When you layer those signals on top of each other, you start to see momentum. Maps help me demonstrate that to collaborators quickly.

## What it shows right now

- **Institutions:** UK DSIT, EU AI Act working groups, Canadian Treasury Board.
- **Signals:** policy papers, grant announcements, testimony, pilot programs.
- **Tags:** compute, safety, procurement, civic tech.

## What’s next

I'm adding alerts so newsletter readers can subscribe to slices of the map (for example, “compute + municipal”). If you have public data I should ingest, please reach out.
